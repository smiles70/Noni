========================================
THE TWO THINGS STILL BLOCKING SERIES B
========================================

BLOCKER 1: ONE DATABASE = TOTAL OUTAGE IF IT FAILS
-- Why it matters: This is the #1 question in technical due diligence. "What happens if your database goes down?" If the answer is "total outage," that is a flag or disqualification.
-- Fix: Fly.io Managed Postgres (automatic failover) or manually building a high-availability cluster
-- Effort: High

BLOCKER 2: NO FRONTEND ERROR REPORTING
-- Why it matters: "How do you know when users hit bugs?" If the answer is "we don't," you are flying blind. You also cannot diagnose paid user issues.
-- Fix: Sentry (free tier handles 5,000 events per month -- more than enough for Noni's current scale)
-- Effort: Low

CLOSE BOTH = +22 POINTS -> 543/720
Still Stage 4, but the last critical reds are gone. Noni becomes a "clean" Series A story with a clear path to B.
