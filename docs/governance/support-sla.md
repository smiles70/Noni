# Support SLA

**Version:** 1.0.0
**Effective Date:** 2026-05-28
**Owner:** Customer Success

---

## Response Time Commitments

| Severity | Definition | Initial Response | Resolution Target |
|:---|:---|:---:|:---:|
| **P0 -- Critical** | Service down; cannot access curriculum; payment failure | 2 hours | 24 hours |
| **P1 -- High** | Major feature degraded; progress not saving | 8 hours | 72 hours |
| **P2 -- Normal** | Questions about curriculum; account issues | 48 hours | 1 week |
| **P3 -- Low** | Feature requests; feedback; general questions | 1 week | No commitment |

---

## Support Channels

| Channel | Availability | Typical Response |
|:---|:---|:---|
| Email: support@mynaani.com | 24/7 (monitored business hours) | Per SLA above |
| In-app feedback form | 24/7 | 1 week |
| Help center | Self-service | Immediate |

---

## Escalation

If your issue is not resolved within the target time, it automatically escalates:

| Level | Escalates To |
|:---|:---|
| P0 not resolved in 24h | Engineering Lead + CEO |
| P1 not resolved in 72h | Engineering Lead |
| P2 not acknowledged in 48h | On-call engineer |

---

## Scope

**In scope:**
- Curriculum content questions
- Technical issues (login, progress, payments)
- Account management (deletion, export)
- Refund requests

**Out of scope:**
- General AI/Claude support (contact Anthropic)
- Device-specific troubleshooting beyond browser compatibility
- Curriculum content customization requests

---

## Refund Processing

| Request Type | Processing Time |
|:---|:---:|
| Standard refund (within 30 days) | 5-10 business days to original payment method |
| Gift refund (purchaser request) | 5-10 business days to purchaser's payment method |

---

## Review

This SLA is reviewed **quarterly** and published at `https://mynaani.com/support`.
