Privacy Policy -- mynaani

Last updated: May 28, 2026

1. What Data We Collect

We collect only the data necessary to provide the mynaani learning experience:

  • Account information: Email address (via Clerk auth). No passwords stored by mynaani.
  • Learning progress: Which curriculum units you have viewed and completed.
  • Telemetry: Anonymous usage patterns to improve the curriculum. No personal content from lessons is stored.
  • Purchase records: If you purchase Modules 4-5, we store purchase status and entitlement.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To save your learning progress across sessions
  • To personalize the curriculum experience
  • To process purchases and gift redemptions
  • To improve the product through anonymized telemetry

We do not sell your data. We do not use your data for advertising.

3. Data Encryption

All data is encrypted in transit using TLS 1.3. Data at rest is encrypted using AES-256 via our database provider (Supabase).

4. Data Retention

We retain your account and progress data until you delete your account. Telemetry data is automatically deleted after 90 days. Purchase records are retained for 7 years for tax compliance.

5. Your Rights

  • Access: You can view your account data in the Account Settings page.
  • Deletion: You can request account deletion at any time via Account Settings. We provide a 7-day grace period during which you can cancel the deletion.
  • Portability: Contact us to export your learning progress.

6. Third Parties

We use the following services:

  • Clerk: Authentication (JWT tokens). Clerk Privacy Policy
  • Stripe: Payment processing. Stripe Privacy Policy
  • Supabase: Database hosting. Supabase Privacy Policy
  • Cloudflare: Hosting and backups. Cloudflare Privacy Policy
